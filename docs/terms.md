## What ChainCheck is

ChainCheck displays winter road conditions, chain control levels, weather
forecasts, and resort snow reports for the Tahoe area, using data published
by Caltrans, the CHP, the National Weather Service, Open-Meteo, and the
resorts themselves. ChainCheck is an independent project and is not
affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any government agency or any
resort.

## The important part: verify before you drive

Mountain conditions change faster than any app can follow. Data can be
wrong, delayed, or missing, and a summary produced by software (including
the AI trip brief) can contain mistakes. ChainCheck never tells you that a
road is safe. Before driving, verify conditions with official sources:
dial 511 in California or check quickmap.dot.ca.gov. Whether and how to
drive is always your decision and your responsibility.
